Polka Dot Cookies

Makes 4-5 dozen 2-inch cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) butter
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1-1/2 cups unsifted all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 cups mint chips

Step by Step

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Line cookie sheet with parchment or use ungreased.
  2. Melt semisweet chips and butter, stirring constantly until smooth. Set aside to cool.
  3. In large bowl beat eggs, sugar, salt and vanilla until light.
  4. Blend in melted chocolate then flour combined with baking soda.
  5. Stir in green mint chips and chill for 10-15 minutes or until stiff.
  6. Drop rounded teaspoonfuls onto cookie sheets.
  7. Bake 8-10 minutes; cookies will be soft in center but will harden as the cool.
